About Us

Margaritas Grill brings the spirit of Mexico's Pacific coast to Nevada — bold ceviches, garlic butter camarones, and platillos meant to be shared around the table.

Our kitchen honors tradition with fresh seafood daily, house-made salsas, and recipes passed through generations.

  • Fresh seafood & house-made salsas
  • Full bar with craft margaritas
  • Family-friendly dining
